The Lil Kemet Land Development Project

The Black Achievement Fund is thrilled to announce that we are building a 6-acre, 20,000 sqft Members' Retreat in Georgia called "Little Kemet." This retreat will be collectively owned and enjoyed by BAF members, and will be the first major asset in our real estate portfolio. The Retreat will feature 14 bedrooms, 16 bathrooms, a commercial kitchen and dining facilities, indoor/outdoor spa and fitness facilities, pool, media room, game room, conference room, art gallery, music studio and more. The retreat will also feature a community farm and greenhouse. The projected cost is $5 - 7 million. We are raising the capital in phases.
